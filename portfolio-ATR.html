All- Terrain Vehicle

Project information

  • Category: Mechatronics
  • Team Size: 4
  • Project date: Jul, 2021 - Nov, 2021

Our ATV project boasted several significant aspects that set it apart. One notable feature was its six-wheel configuration, with two wheels incorporating free hinges, providing enhanced freedom of movement and exceptional balance. We implemented multiple control options, including a Bluetooth mobile app with a user-friendly GUI control pad, allowing for intuitive operation. Additionally, we integrated voice command functionality, enabling hands-free control and adding an extra layer of convenience to the system. These features collectively contributed to the versatility and user-friendly nature of our ATV project.
